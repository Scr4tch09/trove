What we stand for

Three rules shape everything on this site:

  • Hand-picked, not scraped. Every product on TrovePicks was chosen deliberately.
  • Fewer, better picks. We would rather list one great water bottle than thirty mediocre ones.
  • Honesty about how we earn. TrovePicks is funded through affiliate links.

Why you can trust our picks

Before a product earns a spot on TrovePicks, it has to clear a few hurdles:

  • Cross-checked reviews. We read reviews across multiple shops and communities.
  • Usefulness per euro. A pick has to be clearly better than the cheaper alternative.
  • Would we replace it? If it broke tomorrow, would we buy it again?

We also correct our mistakes. If you bought something on our recommendation and it disappointed you, tell us.
